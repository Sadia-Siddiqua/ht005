Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with organic farming?

Start with small-scale organic practices and gradually scale up. There are many resources available for training and certifications.

What are the best crops to grow in different seasons?

Different crops thrive in different seasons. It is important to plan your planting schedule based on the climate and weather conditions.
