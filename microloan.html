Apply for Microloan for Staple Food Farming

Apply for a zero-interest loan to farm imported staple foods like wheat, onions, garlic, fruits, and vegetables. Farmers cultivating **local products** can also apply with a 3% interest rate. Choose your loan provider (NGO or Bank) and apply for the loan based on your farming needs.
